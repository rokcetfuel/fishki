fishki

Learn a new language on the go

Ever wanted to learn a new language, but found that you just don’t have the time? Never again! With Fishki, you can learn any language on the go in short sessions between your responsibilities. Just select the language you know, and the one you want to learn and you're good!

Simplicity

Not really all that tech-y? That’s not an issue – our app was designed with comfort of use as its biggest priority, it is really easy to navigate and customize.

Adaptability

Are you using a PC, tablet or smartphone? Doesn't matter! Our application will adapt to your device and adjust all settings and views, so you never have to worry about picking one tool for learning.

Accuracy

Our translations are provided by an external API, which is known for its amazing accuracy. All words will be translated as if you’d use a dictionary, no room for mistakes here!

Current features

Version 1.0

  • a) Create multiple sets of Fishki for different languages
  • b) Add, remove or edit Fishki or try our quick translations
  • c) Tag fishki to split them up in categories for the ease of browsing
  • d) Use our special Chinese enchancement - get the pronuciation right away
  • e) Install the app on your phone for quick access based on PWA features
  • f) Hide the answers to test your knowledge
  • g) Get motivated by our wise quotes :)
